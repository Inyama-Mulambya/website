OUR PHILOSOPHY

The Universe is Our Home

"Our skies, a common heritage, remind us that just like the stars, we too have a right to be here. Beyond the horizons, we see our vast cosmic neighbourhood, we reach out to feel and touch infinity through our finite senses. We understand the delicate balance of sun light and moon light, the laws of orbit and gravity, the joy of seasons and weather. STARi Labs reaches out beyond the horizons no less to make the cosmos feel like home."

STARi Labs builds on this philosophy and sets its laboratory among the stars. What for? To claim humanity's place in the cosmos, to pursue what is potentially possible, and to have a cosmic perspective of the good earth. How? By inspiring generations with the wonders of the universe, building remote intelligent systems to effectively monitor and manage our resources on earth, and innovating and researching on the frontiers of engineering and research — Beyond Horizons!
